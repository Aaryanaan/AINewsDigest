About AI News Digest

AI News Digest is a personalized reading feed that uses semantic embeddings to match stories to what you actually care about.

How it works
  1. Ingest. We pull articles from a curated list of RSS feeds.
  2. Embed. Each article is converted into a vector that captures its meaning.
  3. Match. Your interest profile is compared against every article using cosine similarity.
  4. Rank. We blend the match score with a recency boost and limit how many stories come from any single source.
  5. Summarize. Short previews let you skim fast and click through only to what grabs you.
Privacy

Profiles are stored locally alongside articles — nothing is shared with third parties.
